About Nansen Forensics

Our Mission is to deliver the highest quality digital forensic services to our clients, Australia wide. Our team is led by experts with diverse techical and investigative backgrounds, each with decades of experience. This allows us to offer a broad range of professional services aligned with your needs.

Our team consists of a former detective and digital forensic analyst, a former defence Intelligence and cyber security expert and a global blockchain expert. We are always happy to answer your questions, so contact us if you would like to learn more.


Services

We offer a broad range of professional forensic services Australia wide. Why us?

Our team consists of highly trained staff, including ex law enforcement digital forensic analysts and former detectives, cyber security experts and blockchain programmers. These specialist skills support our team's unique capabilities when it comes to delivering results. All staff are highly trained in digital forensics, possessing at minimum industry qualifications in the tools that underpin our work. Most hold additional qualifications at the Degree and Master's levels. We are always happy to answer your questions about our qualifications and experience.
