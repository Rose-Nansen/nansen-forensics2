What we do

We provide a range of traditional digital forensic services, such as mobile and computer forensics as well as contemporary blockchain, cryptocurrency and NFT forensics. We follow suspicious cryptocurrency trades, highlight suspicious activity, alert you to transactions from an address of interest, uncover hidden funds, reverse engineer, track stolen funds and more!

How we work

We provide digital forensic services Australia wide. Our experience has taught us that clients often do not require us on 'scene' and are commonly in possession of the devices they need analysed. What is important to our clients is the provision of professional, reliable and expedient service delivery, without compromise. For this reason we have simplified our Digital Forensic Services by partnering with TNT Courier to manage fail safe, hand to hand pick up and delivery of your devices. The fail safe delivery method satisfies chain of custody requirements so it is suitable for cases where chain of custody is crucial.

'Our model removes the logistical burden and allows you to get work done'.

Our team works hard to deliver our clients high quality personalised services which means we are available to answer your questions, and typically get mobile device jobs done within 48 hours! All our staff are highly qualified and trained, and security cleared to NV1.
